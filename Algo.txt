Step 0: Assess hunger levels to determine necessity of pizza.
Step 1: Assess energy levels to determine whether pizza will be made or ordered. If no, skip to step 2b.

Step 2a: Retrieve pizza dough from the fridge.
Step 3a: Add Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ sauce to center of dough and spread toward edges.
Step 4a: Chop rotisserie chicken into 1/2 inch chunks and spread over surface.
Step 5a: Spread mozarella and asiago cheese over surface to edge of sauce ring.
Step 6a: Dice 1/2 small white onion and spread over cheese.
Step 7a: Spread cooked chopped bacon or bacon bits to taste.
Step 8a: Preheat oven to 451 degrees fahrenheit with pizza stone inside.
Step 9a: Place prepped pizza on cooking stone and leave in oven for 13 and 1/2 minutes.
Step 10a: Remove and cut pizza into squares or triangles according to preference.
